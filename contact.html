About RIDEVISE

Our project, RIDEVISE, is designed to empower users with the smartest choices for their daily short- or medium-distance commutes. In today's digital market, multiple platforms like metro services, Ola, Uber, and Rapido offer transportation options. RIDEVISE brings formula-based fare estimates from these providers—across various vehicle types—onto a single page, simplifying comparisons based on price and CO₂ emissions (which vary by vehicle).

We address the hassle of price hunting for cost-conscious users while raising awareness about the environmental footprint of travel decisions. By highlighting how each commute impacts the planet, we encourage greener choices. For shorter distances, we promote walking as a healthy, zero-emission alternative, tracking users' walking progress and estimating calories burned to motivate better habits.

All your commute choices are saved, providing insightful analytics on spending patterns, carbon emissions, and physical activity—all in one seamless platform. Join us in making commuting smarter, cheaper, and more sustainable!
